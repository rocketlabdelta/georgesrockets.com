Space Shuttle Information

Drawings
  • ICD - Interface Control Documents - Drawings of the ET, SRB's, and some orbiter info. From 1991.

Photos

  • The following Photos were compiled for two purposes. One was to specifically document STS-47, of which I built a flying 1/72 model of. The other was to help to document shuttles in general. In late 2001, I put all of them together into a CD that I sent out to a number of people. I have not updated any photos here since then.

 

STS-47 - Endeavour

Photos of STS-47, orbiter Endeavour.

 

Endeavour not STS-047

Photos of Endeavour on other missions (prior to end of 2002). Includes STS-49

 

ET Photos

Photos either of the ET by itself or good views of details on the ET.

 

SRB

Photos either of SRB components or good detail views of SRB's.

 

Orbiter

Photos of either orbiters themselves or detail views. Also landing photos.

 

SSME Engines

Photos of the Space Shuttle Main Engines themselves or detail views.

 

Struts & Interfaces

Photos showing good views of where the Orbiter or SRB's attach to the ET. Not all-inclusive.

 

Stacking

Shuttles being assembled (stacked) inside the VAB.

 

Stack Closeups

Close views of shuttle stacks (assembled shuttle on pad), showing decent to good detail.

 

Shuttle On Pad

More views of shuttles on pads

 

Launches

Some launch photos that are large and detailed, some small.

 

MSFC

Some REALLY nice photos from Marshall Space Flight Center's "MIX" Photo galleries.

 

STS-91 Lite ET

First launch with the Super LightWeight Tank

 

STS-95_Glenn

A few photos of the mission that John Glenn flew in 1998

 

STS-98_ISS

February 2001 mission to the International Space Station.

Some neat photos of Atlantis coming in to dock.

 

STS-102 SRB Sea Recovery

Ocean views of an SRB landing in the Atlantic, and underwater photos preparing for retrieval.

 

Florida Today

Used to be a fantastic site for info and photos of shuttles being prepared for missions. Pics here are ones I'm sure came from that site before it changed.

 

747 Shuttle

Photos of shuttle orbiters with 747 Shuttle Carrier Aircraft.

 

Other

Shuttle items that do not quite fit elsewhere but usually worth taking a look at.
