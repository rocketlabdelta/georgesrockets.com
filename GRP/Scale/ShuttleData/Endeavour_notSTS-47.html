Endeavour is the 5th operational shuttle orbiter. Built to replace Challenger.

Due to a schedule quirk, Endeavour's first mission was STS-49, then it flew STS-47.

Endeavour 1992 Patch
 

STS-49

As always there are minor differences on ET's and even SRB's. Particularly the placement and use of insta-foam (lighter color orange or even yellow) on portions of the ET and even SRB's

Also, some details of the same mission as-seen on rollout are different by the time of liftoff. Some tiles are left out for servicing work on the pad, including opening and closing of the bay doors.
