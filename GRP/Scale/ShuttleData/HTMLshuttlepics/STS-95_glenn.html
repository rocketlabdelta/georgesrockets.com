A few photos of the 1998 Discovery mission that John Glenn flew aboard.

Discovery's first mission after changing the markings to the NASA "Meatball".

Elevon upper hinge seals also changed from dark gray to near-white in color.

Mission also used a light ET.

The "sunburning" effect can be seen, where the ET areas exposed the most to sunlight is darker and ET shaded by the pad's RSS is lighter in color.

The last 6 images are small. Larger ones (and captions) can be obtained by going to the following url and entering in the last 4 digits into the "xxxx"

http://images.ksc.nasa.gov/cgi-bin/
find-image?KSC-98PC-xxxx&html
