ICD stands for Interface Control Document. It defines the external surface of a launch vehicle.

Jimmy Williams obtained an ICD with a great deal of information on the shuttle ET and SRB's. Most of the drawings here are scans from that ICD. There are still many pages from that ICD which have not been scanned yet.

A few other pages are from a Rockwell ICD defining portions of the orbiter.
