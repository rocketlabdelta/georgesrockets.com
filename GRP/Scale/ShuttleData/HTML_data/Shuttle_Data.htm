Shuttle Data


Shuttle data that's beenscanned, obtained via the web, or created in MacDraw

Many people have helped in obtaining data on the shuttle throughout the years.

Such as Jimmy Williams, John Pursley, Peter Alway, Rob Justis, Matt Steele, Jack Strieter, Mike Mackowski, and various folks on internet or mail-list groups.

A lot of the printee data has not been scanned yet, however a lot of the ICD for the ET & SRB's has been done.

 
GCG Drawings - Some of George's MacDraw drawings, converted to GIF's

ICD Data - Scans from the shuttle ICD (Interface Control Document) set.

Other Data - Printed drawings from sources other than ICD's above or Shuttle Operational Databook below.
