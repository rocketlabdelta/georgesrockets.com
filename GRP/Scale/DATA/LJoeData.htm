Little Joe II Drawings
by George Gassaway & Tom Beach

The following Little Joe-II Drawings were mostly done by George. Some portions were done by Tom. The Pad Drawings were entirely Tom's.

Most of these drawings were in the May/June and July/August 1991 issues of American Spacemodeling Magazine (The NAR's magazine name before Sport Rocketry).
