Concorde

(1980)

I created a Sport Glider model of the Concorde, based on a BT-50. It used an internal pop-pod much like the Estes SkyDart.

It was published as a plan in the July, 1980 issue of the NAR's magazine, The Model Rocketeer.

It was later scaled up as an HPR kit. It was renamed the "SST", and sold by Wasatch Rocketry without any acknowledgment that it was an upscale of this plan.
