  TalladegaRace - Oct 5, 2008

 

Several members of BRB went to the Amp Energy 500 NASCAR race at Talladega, car-pooling with Richard Fernandez.

We had expected to have a long delay getting in due to traffic, but it was not bad at all (we passed the West exit off of I-20 and came in from the East side). We got there at 10:30 AM.

Our seats were in the O.V. Hill North "Tower" section (upper deck)

David Rowe had been to a couple of NASCAR races many years ago, in Atlanta. For the rest of us, it was our first NASCAR race of any kind. We all enjoyed it a lot.
