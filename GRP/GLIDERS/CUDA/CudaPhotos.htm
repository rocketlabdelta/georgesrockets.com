2000 WSMC

Slovakia

Chinese Junior S8 Team

Three Chinese Junior Cudas

Close-Up

For the 2000 WSMC, the Chinese Team bought Stingray kits for their Senior Team, and Cuda kits for their Junior Team.

One mod they made was to mount the tail upright, instead of inverted. This made them look a bit like the Estes Sweet Vee.

They boosted OK, but the upright V-tail was in the very turbulent and draggy exhaust zone, so it cost some altitude.
