National Association of Rocketry - Sport Rocketry organization founded in 1957. Covering Model Rocketry and High Power, with local clubs, launches, insurance, a magazine, newsletter, special publications, HPR certification, etc. For more information go to their web page.

USA Spacemodeling - Site about FAI (Federation Aeronautique Internationale) Rocketry competition and the US Team which is chosen every 2 years to compete in the World Spacemodeling Championships (WSMC).
Other groups:

  • NARScale Home Page - Special Interest Group (recognized by the NAR) dedicated to encouragement of building and flying of Scale models by the sharing of methods and data. This includes a dedicated E-mail list.
    • ScaleRoc E-Mail List - Join the NARscale E-mail list.
  • SEP - Student Experimental Payload Program - A non-profit organization dedicated to hands-on aerospace education, run by Greg Warren.
  • HARA - Huntsville Area Rocketry Association  - Northern Alabama NAR and TRA club holding regular launches.
  • Casting E-mail list - Subscribe to a mail-list on making masters and molds for casting model parts
